About

This project is an attempt to widening my gamut of music through archiving music not readily available on the major streaming platforms like Spotify + iTunes.

The goal of this project is not to exclusively contain music that is not on major platforms, but instead is to collect music from a wide range of untraditional sources which a regular playlists would be unable to facilitate.

Please upload music that you have collected throughout your life that may not appear on streaming services using this as a platform for cherishing and archiving what may fade away.
